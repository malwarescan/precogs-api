# Risk Scoring Guidelines

## Risk Levels

- **Low (0.0-0.3)**: Safe to DIY, no immediate danger
- **Medium (0.3-0.6)**: Professional recommended, monitor closely
- **High (0.6-0.8)**: Professional required, potential safety issues
- **Critical (0.8-1.0)**: Emergency, immediate professional intervention needed

## Triage Levels

- **safe**: Low risk, can wait
- **monitor**: Medium risk, schedule soon
- **urgent**: High risk, schedule immediately
- **emergency**: Critical risk, call professional now

## Hazard Assessment

- **No hazards**: Safe conditions
- **Minor hazards**: Be cautious
- **Moderate hazards**: Professional recommended
- **Severe hazards**: Professional required immediately
